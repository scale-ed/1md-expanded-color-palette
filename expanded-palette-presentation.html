Expanded Color Palette — Six Directions for Discussion

Six palette directions for extending 1MD's brand system into high-saturation social ads — a fixed, reusable palette the media team can pull from consistently, instead of one-off color choices on every new ad request.

Brief: Media buyers want to break from brand guidelines with highly saturated colors (a bold bright green in particular) to stop the scroll on Meta. Brand wants guardrails so ads don't fragment into one-off palettes per request. This system is the compromise — a fixed, reusable "loud" palette instead of ad-hoc color choices, still anchored to 1MD's Light Elderberry navy and neutrals so it reads as the brand at a glance.
